About Phoenix Builds

Phoenix Builds is a premier flooring and renovation company based in New Zealand, dedicated to transforming spaces with quality craftsmanship and exceptional materials.

With years of experience in the industry, our team of skilled professionals takes pride in delivering outstanding results that exceed our clients' expectations.

We believe that the foundation of any beautiful space starts from the ground up, which is why we specialize in premium flooring solutions alongside comprehensive renovation services.
